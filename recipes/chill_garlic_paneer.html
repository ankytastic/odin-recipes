Chill Garlic Paneer

Description

Chill Garlic Paneer is a delightful and flavorful Indian appetizer featuring crispy paneer cubes tossed in a spicy and tangy garlic sauce. Perfect for any gathering or as a delicious snack.

Ingredients

  • Paneer: Fresh paneer cubes work best for this recipe.
  • Garlic: Minced garlic is the key to the sauce's flavor.
  • Soy Sauce: Adds a savory depth to the dish.
  • Chili Sauce: Provides a spicy kick.
  • Vinegar: For a touch of acidity and tanginess.
  • Green Onions: For garnish and added flavor.
  • Seasonings: Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Flour and Cornstarch: For coating the paneer before frying.
  • Oil: For frying the paneer cubes.

How to Make Chill Garlic Paneer

  1. Prepare the paneer by coating it in a mixture of flour and cornstarch, then fry until golden and crispy.
  2. In a pan, saut√© minced garlic until fragrant, then add soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar.
  3. Toss the fried paneer in the garlic sauce until well-coated.
  4. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
