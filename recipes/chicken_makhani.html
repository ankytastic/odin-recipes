Chicken Makhani

Description

Chicken Makhani, also known as Butter Chicken, is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a luscious tomato and butter sauce. It's perfect when paired with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken: Boneless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into pieces.
  • Butter: For a rich and creamy base.
  • Tomatoes: Use tomato puree for a smooth sauce.
  • Heavy Cream: Adds creaminess to the sauce.
  • Garlic and Ginger: Minced for flavor and aroma.
  • Spices: Garam masala, cumin, coriander, and turmeric.
  • Salt and Pepper: For seasoning.
  • Cilantro: Fresh cilantro for garnish.

How to Make Chicken Makhani

  1. Marinate the chicken in a mixture of yogurt and spices, then cook it until fully cooked.
  2. In a separate pan, cook garlic and ginger in butter, then add tomato puree and spices to make the sauce.
  3. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer until the flavors meld.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and cook until the sauce is creamy and rich.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with naan or rice.
